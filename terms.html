Terms & Conditions

Please read our policies carefully regarding orders, returns, payments, and delays.

🔄

Return Policy

We accept product returns only if the product is not working. Please inspect your item immediately upon delivery and report any functionality issues to us.

💳

Advance Payment

For orders exceeding Rs. 10,000, a 50% advance payment is mandatory. The remaining 50% can be paid as Cash on Delivery.

⏳

Delay Compensation

If the delivery of your product is delayed by more than 31 days from the order date, we will pay you a compensation of Rs. 200 per day for every additional day of delay.

❌

Order Cancellation

You can cancel your order at any time up to 1 day (24 hours) from the time the order was placed. Cancellations are not accepted after this period.
